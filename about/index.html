About Dyer’s Cabinet Shop

Dyer’s Cabinet Shop started in 1975 when Ed Dyer began designing and making custom furniture in his shop next to his home in Plumsted Twp. He eventually began making high quality, custom kitchen cabinetry and fabricating his own laminate and Corian countertops. In 1989, he took on Aristokraft and Decora’s cabinet lines.

In 1998, Ed, his wife Kathy, and their sons Eddie and Mike, who grew up in the business, moved their shop to Rt. 528 Lakewood Road, in New Egypt. The business grew and they stopped making their own custom cabinets. They took on two custom lines, Jay Rambo and Grabill Cabinets. They also bought in granite and quartz countertops.

Dyer’s Cabinet Shop opened it’s present showroom at 415 Rt. 539, Cream Ridge, NJ in January of 2006. The beautiful new showroom features displays of all their lines as well as a variety of granite, quartz and Corian countertops and hardware displays.
